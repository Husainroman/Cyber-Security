What is Certificate Transparency?

Under Certificate Transparency(CT), a Certificate Authority will have to publish all SSL/TLS they issue in a public logs. 


Anyone can look through the CT logs and find certificate issued for a domain.

Website : http://www.certificate-transparency.org/known-logs


You can enter an Identity Domain name, Organization name etc..

Website : https://crt.sh

Certificate Transparency crt.sh Automation : Bash Script to parse the results and save output in a file.


Shodan Subdomain Enumeration : shodan.io aggregates data collected as a part of scans project shodan.io aggregates  SSL/TLS certificate and let us search through them. Thus, it potentially helps us to discover a new subdomain.
